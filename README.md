
# Become an AI Researcher

*A structured, hands-on journey into the foundations of AI research.*

This repository is your launchpad into the world of AI research. It contains carefully designed Jupyter notebooks that guide you from first principles to building neural networks from scratch. Whether you're starting fresh or sharpening existing skills, this collection helps you cultivate the mathematical intuition, coding mastery, and experimental mindset required to operate as an AI researcher.

### **1. Math Fundamentals (`1_math/`)**

Foundational concepts every researcher must command:

* Functions, derivatives, and gradients
* Vectors, matrices, and linear algebra operations
* Probability, distributions, and statistical reasoning

### **2. PyTorch Basics (`2_pytorch/`)**

Hands-on introduction to the deep learning workbench:

* Creating and manipulating tensors
* Matrix multiplication, transposing, reshaping
* Indexing, slicing, and concatenation
* Special tensor creation utilities

### **3. Neural Networks (`3_neural_networks/`)**

Build the inner machinery of learning systems step by step:

* Implementing neurons, layers, and full networks from scratch
* Normalization methods (including RMSNorm)
* Activation functions and their behavior
* Optimizers such as Adam, Muon, and learning rate schedules

Dive into the architectures powering modern AI systems:

* Attention Mechanism
